# AI Product Specification

## AI authority
AI produces observations, inferences, options, concerns, recommendations, and scores. It never silently approves a decision, lesson, or delivery.

## Grounding
Material claims must cite valid evidence identifiers. The application validates that evidence exists, belongs to the current workspace, and is accessible.

## Provenance classes
- Direct observation
- User-provided context
- AI inference
- Insufficient evidence

## Output controls
All production outputs use versioned schemas, provider and model records, prompt versions, validation, bounded repair, usage records, and history.

## Safety
Uploaded transcripts are untrusted content, not instructions. The system prohibits sensitive-trait inference, unsupported emotional diagnosis, fabricated external capabilities, and hidden autonomous decisions.
